TNT20 Token Generator

Welcome. On this site, you can generate your own token (for now in privatenet) with your wallet in Chrome, without the need for technical knowledge.

If you don't have the Theta wallet extension installed in Chrome, click here.


Step 1

Connect your wallet (if this is your first time, you must authorize this website to get access to your wallet)


Step 2

Step 3 (optional)


Check your account (you need TFUEL for creating your new token)


Step 4
